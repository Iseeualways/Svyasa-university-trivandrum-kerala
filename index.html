ABOUT US

S-VYASA University (Deemed-to-be University), Trivandrum, Kerala

Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana, popularly known as S-VYASA University, is a UGC-approved Deemed-to-be University. As part of its ongoing efforts to achieve comprehensive growth across various domains of academics and co-curricular activities, the university has established a new campus in the Evergreen City of India, i.e., Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala.

The primary goal of this expansion is to extend the university's objective of achieving academic excellence beyond geographical boundaries. Moreover, through a distinctive blend of traditional learning and the modern methods of pedagogy, the university aims to nurture aspirants into ideal leaders of the future.
